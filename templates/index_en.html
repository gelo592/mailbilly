FAQ

What kind of text can I send to MailBilly?

Any communication less than 600 words, either English that you would like improved or French that you would like translated into English. For larger or more technical texts, please contact us directly for a devis.

What kind of quality can I expect with Mail Billy?

We go well beyond simply ensuring that the English in your e-mail is correct. Our communication experts inject personality, charm, professionalism, and eloquence into your message.

Who are these so-called experts?

These are U.S. and U.K.-born individuals who have at least some professional background in marketing or communication, whether it be higher education or on-the-job experience.

What about confidentiality?

We recommend substituting names of people, companies, or organizations with aliases if you’re concerned about confidentiality. We are also happy to sign a confidentiality agreement with you.

What about word count?

You can calculate the number of words in your message here. Based on the number of words you can see which price category the e-mail falls in.

What are the hours of operation for Mail Billy?

While in beta, we guarantee our one-hour service from 9H to 23H (UTC + 01:00) Monday through Sunday. Our next big upgrade will feature 24/7 service.

Do you do English to French translation?

Yes, but it’s guaranteed within the day, not the hour.

Is Billy single?

Shoot him a message and ask for yourself! ;)

What can we expect in the post-beta version of Mail Billy?

Oh gosh, so many swell things. A browser-based interface with email tracking. A timer so you’ll know approximately when you can expect to receive your email.

Future versions of the service will include an e-mail plug-in/widget to make communicating with Billy even easier, as well as a Slack integration. We’ll also have a built-in confidentiality enforcer that automatically scrambles proper nouns (people, places, companies, etc.) in your emails before our experts receive them.
